# Reset tokens could be brute-forced or replayed

## Mitigation
High-entropy tokens, short expiry, single-use enforcement (T-0102). Residual risk
low once lockout policy (Q-0005) is decided.

## Early-warning signal
Spike in reset-confirm failures per account — watch in the verification loop.
