# A dispatcher wants work orders auto-scheduled to the best-fit technician

As a **dispatcher**, I want **work orders automatically assigned to the optimal
technician by skills, location, and availability**, so that I **reduce travel
time and stop hand-scheduling hundreds of jobs a day**.
