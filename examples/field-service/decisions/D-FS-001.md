# Use RSO instead of a custom scheduler

## Context
R-FS-001 needs constraint-based optimization at scale.

## Decision
Adopt Resource Scheduling Optimization (RSO).

## Alternatives considered
1. **Custom Azure optimizer (TL-custom-scheduler)** — max control, but high build
   and maintenance cost, and lock-in. Rejected.
2. **Manual only** — does not scale (see CS-FS-001). Rejected.

## Consequences
- Positive: supported, fast to configure, integrates with Field Service.
- Negative: licensing cost; optimization is a configured black box.
- Reversibility: moderate (config, not code).
